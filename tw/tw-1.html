Lionel Messi

Lionel Messi is widely regarded as one of the greatest football players of all time. The Argentine forward has won a record seven Ballon d'Or awards.

Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo is a Portuguese professional footballer with five Ballon d'Or awards. He has achieved success in England, Spain, and Italy.

Kylian Mbappé

Kylian Mbappé is a French forward known for his explosive speed and clinical finishing. He won the World Cup with France in 2018.

Kevin De Bruyne

Kevin De Bruyne is a Belgian midfielder celebrated for his exceptional vision and passing range. He has been instrumental for Manchester City.

Erling Haaland

Erling Haaland is a Norwegian striker known for his incredible goal-scoring prowess and physical presence.

Neymar Jr

Neymar Jr is a Brazilian forward known for his exceptional dribbling skills, creativity, and flair.

Mohamed Salah

Mohamed Salah is an Egyptian forward known for his speed, finishing, and playmaking abilities. He has been crucial for Liverpool's success.

Robert Lewandowski

Robert Lewandowski is a Polish striker known for his clinical finishing and positioning. He has been one of Europe's top scorers for years.
