Sligo Animal Rescue

Compassion in action for every creature

Emergency Animal Rescue

We respond to urgent calls to rescue injured, abandoned, or mistreated animals throughout County Sligo.

Our trained volunteers work closely with vets and authorities to ensure every animal receives immediate care.

Rehabilitation & Foster Care

Animals in distress are placed in loving foster homes where they receive medical treatment and emotional support.

We provide food, supplies, and guidance to foster families to help animals recover and thrive.

Adoption & Rehoming Services

Once rehabilitated, our animals are matched with caring adopters through a thorough screening and support process.
